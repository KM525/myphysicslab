Simulates a pendulum attached to another body with a spring. The pendulum is a rigid body with a pivot attached to the background.

Includes demonstration of drawing a graph to show the movement of a body as trailing lines following the body. Also demonstrates automatically zooming the view to show the graph as it is drawn.
